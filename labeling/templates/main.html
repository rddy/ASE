In this experiment, you will recognize pictures of handwritten digits (0-9).
Each picture will be broken down into a sequence of partial pictures, like the picture of a '3' below.

The sequence slowly builds up the full picture, one row of pixels at a time.
Pixels that have not yet been revealed are white, while pixels that have been revealed to be blank are gray.
You will be shown each partial picture in the sequence, one at a time.
Please label each partial picture with the digit that you think it represents.
If the partial picture is empty or does not contain enough detail for you to tell what digit it is, please mark "Not sure".
After clicking "Submit", more of the picture will be revealed, and you will have a chance to update your label.
After the full picture has been revealed, a new sequence of partial pictures (of a potentially different digit) will be shown.
